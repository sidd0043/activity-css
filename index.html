48 Million Reasons to Smilies for the Young 21 year Old Neymar

Brazil striker Neymar completed his move from Santos to Barcelona for £48.6m (57m euros) on Monday.

Neymar's father said Tuesday that Barcelona paid his company 10 million euros ($13.3 million) in 2011 to guarantee the right to sign the talented Brazilian player.

Neymar's Thoughts on the Transfer

"I'm one of the luckiest men in the world to be able to play with him(Messi) and it's an honour. I'm very happy to be realising my life's dream."
